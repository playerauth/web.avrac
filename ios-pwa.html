Install Avrac

Add to home screen · Quick setup (IOS)

1
Visit the web app
Open the link below to launch Avrac in your browser. Make sure you're using Safari for best results.
avrac.playerauth7.workers.dev
Open in Safari (required for home screen)
2
Tap share icon
In Safari, locate the share button at the bottom of the screen (center toolbar).
Share button bottom bar
It looks like a square with an arrow pointing upward.
3
Add to Home Screen
Scroll down in the share menu and tap “Add to Home Screen”.
Add to Home Screen install option
4
Confirm installation
A preview window will appear with the Avrac icon. Tap “Add” in the upper right corner.
Tap Add button final step
✓
Ready to use
The Avrac icon now appears on your device home screen. Tap it anytime to open the app.
instant launch

always updated
